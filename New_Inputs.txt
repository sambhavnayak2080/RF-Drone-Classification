The target dataset is the "Non-standard Waveforms from Hovering UAVs" (Genesys Lab).

Objective: RF fingerprinting of 7 identical DJI M100 UAVs to distinguish specific devices (not just types).

Format: SigMF (Signal Metadata Format).

Data: Binary .bin files containing interleaved I/Q samples encoded as float16.

Metadata: .json files containing capture details (UAV ID, distance, burst ID, transmission number).

Content:

Classes: 7 UAVs (uav1 to uav7).

Conditions: Hovering at distances of 6ft, 9ft, 12ft, and 15ft.

Signal Structure: Short, non-overlapping bursts separated from noise. Average length ~92k samples per burst.
